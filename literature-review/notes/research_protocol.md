## Inclusion Criteria

- Published within the selected date range.
- Peer-reviewed or academically credible source.

- Duplicate record.
- Full text unavailable.
- Not academically credible.

## Screening Process

1. Record search results in `metadata/search_log.csv`.
2. Add candidate papers to `metadata/papers_master.csv`.
3. Remove or mark duplicates in `metadata/duplicate_check.csv`.
4. Screen title and abstract in `screening/title_abstract_screening.csv`.
5. Screen full text in `screening/full_text_screening.csv`.
6. Extract evidence in `screening/extraction_matrix.csv`.
